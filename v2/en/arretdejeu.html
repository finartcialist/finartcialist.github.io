ADSR: a study.

It's early 2021 and all that everyone is talking about is the story of how a discussion board banded together around a specific stock.

The more I know the less I understand...

This is an artistic interpretation of the quantitative facts, an interpretation that leads, in the piece called 'deuxième ronde', to a 22min long movement, based around a single note.

a simple, overwhelming sound.

this work was generated in a deterministic way, using a data reading script written in Python, followed by a sound making script in Csound.
