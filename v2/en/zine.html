The zine series mouvements sous-jacents (in English, "underlying movements") goals are to detail, explain and bring some context to the artistic process of the finartcialist project.

Made of articles discussing works done under the finartcialist umbrella and presenting works done by other artists and collectives, these zines allows one to follow in depth the evolution of the finartcialist project.

Each zine contains a detailed list of works, projects and articles covering art and finance topics.
